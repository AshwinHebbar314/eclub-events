Out of The Box

Out of The Box is a one of a kind case study competition, it gives the opportunity to learn in a high pressure situation involving a real-world scenario on business aspects of a product. This competition gives you the scope to assess your performance and decision making skill.

Guidelines for Out of the Box

  • Anyone enrolled in the BS Degree Program can participate.
  • You can participate individually or in a team of a maximum of 3 members.
  • No participant can be a part of more than one team.
  • No participant is allowed to switch teams mid-contest.

Registration link:

Click Here

Timeline: Out of the Box

Registration deadline: 10th August 11:59 PM

Google from will be rolled out on 10th August.

Round 1: 11th August to 13th August

In this round, each team will be sent a one-pager PDF containing details about the partnering company and the problem statement. The teams have to cover their proposed solution in a 5-slide presentation.

The top 8 teams will be selected.


Round 2: 15th August

The top 8 teams will deliver the online case presentation explaining their own mitigation strategies / solutions to the company, whose problem statement they are solving.

Interaction with

Entrepreneurs

Interaction with Entrepreneurs is a unique event where the participants get to interact with individuals with the industry. Participants will be introduced to the fundamentals of entrepreneurship development so they can understand the requirements and challenges in this area. This event is open to all IITMBS students.

About the Event

The need of the hour is to develop effective policies to cater to the new challenges such that modern-day problems can be solved. The workshop focuses on the basic and advanced approaches of entrepreneurial development and how they can be imbibed to the participants.
